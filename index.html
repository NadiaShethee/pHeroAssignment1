When you buy a website theme, you are given a disk space limit. This limit is the amount of storage you have in your account. When you exceed this limit, your ability to upload images, videos, or documents will be limited to none. As your disk space limit depends on the size of the files you upload, your choice is to make sure that all of the necessary files on your site do not exceed the limit.
